Inspection & Verification Service

Ensure you're getting exactly what you pay for with our comprehensive vehicle inspection and verification process.

Thorough Vehicle Inspections

At Nick Motors, we understand that buying a vehicle sight-unseen can be daunting. Our professional inspection service gives you peace of mind by thoroughly examining every aspect of your potential purchase before you commit.

Our certified inspectors conduct 150+ point checks on each vehicle, covering mechanical, electrical, body, and interior components. We don't just look for current issues - we identify potential future problems that could cost you money down the road.

Every inspection includes detailed photographs and a comprehensive report with our professional assessment. We'll tell you not just what's wrong with a vehicle, but how significant each issue is and what it might cost to repair.

Our Inspection Services

Pre-Purchase Inspection

Our most popular service - a complete evaluation of any vehicle you're considering purchasing. We'll give you an honest assessment of the vehicle's condition and help you make an informed decision.

Includes:

  • Engine performance evaluation
  • Transmission test
  • Brake system inspection
  • Suspension assessment
  • Electrical systems check
  • Body and frame inspection
  • Undercarriage examination
  • Interior condition evaluation
  • Test drive assessment
  • OBD-II diagnostic scan

Post-Purchase Verification

For vehicles already purchased at auction, we conduct a detailed verification to ensure the vehicle matches its description and grading. This service is included with all our vehicle purchases.

Includes:

  • Verification against auction sheet
  • Mileage confirmation
  • Option package verification
  • Hidden damage detection
  • Undisclosed repair identification
  • Mechanical condition confirmation

Pre-Shipment Inspection

Conducted just before your vehicle ships to ensure no damage has occurred during preparation and storage. Includes detailed photographic documentation of the vehicle's condition.
