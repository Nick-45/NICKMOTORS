Vehicle Customization

Personalize your vehicle to match your style and needs before it ships from Japan.

Tailored to Your Preferences

At Nick Motors, we understand that your vehicle should reflect your personality and meet your specific requirements. Our customization service allows you to personalize your vehicle before it leaves Japan, often at lower costs than doing modifications locally.

Our team works with trusted workshops and parts suppliers across Japan to perform a wide range of customizations. Whether you're looking for performance upgrades, aesthetic enhancements, or functional modifications, we can coordinate the work to your specifications.

All customization work is performed by certified technicians using high-quality parts, and we provide complete documentation of all modifications made to your vehicle.

Our Customization Process

  1. Consultation: We discuss your customization ideas and provide professional recommendations.
  2. Quotation: You receive a detailed quote for all requested modifications.
  3. Approval: Once approved, we schedule the work with our specialized workshops.
  4. Execution: Certified technicians perform the modifications using quality parts.
  5. Quality Control: We inspect all work and provide you with before/after documentation.
  6. Shipping: Your customized vehicle is prepared for shipping to your location.
