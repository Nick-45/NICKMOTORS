Frequently Asked Questions

How long after purchase can I access after-sale support?

We provide lifetime after-sale support for all vehicles purchased through Nick Motors. While some services may have associated fees after the first year, we're always available to assist with documentation and general inquiries.

Can you help me find a local mechanic familiar with Japanese imports?

Yes! We maintain a network of recommended service providers in many countries and can often connect you with a qualified mechanic in your area who has experience with Japanese vehicles.

What if I need parts that aren't available in my country?

We can source genuine OEM parts directly from Japan and ship them to you. Our parts sourcing service is available to all Nick Motors customers, often at better prices than local dealerships.

How do I access my vehicle's maintenance history from Japan?

For vehicles that came with maintenance records, we keep digital copies on file and can provide them upon request. For auction vehicles, we can often obtain available history reports.

What should I do if I'm having trouble registering my imported vehicle?

Contact our support team immediately. We've helped hundreds of customers through the registration process and can provide guidance and documentation to resolve most issues.
