Our Story

Founded in 2010, Nick Motors has grown from a small local dealership to a premier destination for luxury and performance vehicles. What began as a passion project for automotive enthusiast Nick Reynolds has evolved into a trusted name in the industry.

With our headquarters in Detroit and showrooms in three states, we combine hometown values with world-class automotive expertise. Our team of 45 professionals brings over 200 years of combined experience to serve your vehicle needs.

Why Choose Nick Motors?

We're not just another car dealership. Our concierge approach means we take the time to understand your unique needs, whether you're a collector seeking a rare classic or a family looking for the perfect SUV.

Integrity

We believe in transparent pricing and honest assessments. No hidden fees, no pressure tactics - just straightforward advice.

Expertise

Our certified technicians and sales professionals undergo continuous training to stay ahead of industry trends.

Community
