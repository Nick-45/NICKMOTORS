Typical Financing Requirements

While requirements vary by lender, most will ask for:

  • Completed loan application
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements)
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Proof of residence
  • Information about the vehicle you're purchasing
  • Down payment (typically 10-30% for imports)

Our specialists can advise you on how to present your application in the best possible light to improve your approval chances and secure better terms.

Our Financing Process

  1. Pre-Qualification: We assess your needs and financial situation to match you with appropriate lenders.
  2. Application: We help you complete and submit your loan application with all required documentation.
  3. Approval: We follow up with lenders to expedite the approval process.
  4. Terms Review: We explain all loan terms and conditions to ensure you understand your commitment.
  5. Funding: Once approved, funds are disbursed directly to Nick Motors to complete your purchase.
  6. Repayment: You make monthly payments directly to the lender according to your agreed terms.
