Shipping Methods

Container Shipping

Best for: High-value vehicles, luxury cars, classic/collector vehicles

Your vehicle is secured in a 20ft or 40ft steel container, providing maximum protection from the elements and potential damage during transit.

  • 20ft container: 1-2 vehicles
  • 40ft container: 2-4 vehicles
  • Complete protection from weather and sea spray
  • Reduced risk of damage during handling
  • Private, secure storage during transit

Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo)

Best for: Standard vehicles, budget-conscious shipments

Your vehicle is driven onto specialized car carrier ships and secured for ocean transit. The most economical way to ship vehicles internationally.

  • Most cost-effective shipping method
  • Frequent departures to major ports worldwide
  • Suitable for drivable vehicles
  • Basic insurance included

Air Freight

Best for: Urgent shipments, high-value vehicles where speed is essential

The fastest way to get your vehicle overseas, with transit times measured in days rather than weeks.

  • Fastest international delivery (3-7 days)
  • Highest level of security and handling
  • Ideal for time-sensitive shipments
  • Available to most international airports

Our Shipping Process

  1. Documentation Preparation: We handle all export/import paperwork including Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, and Certificate of Origin.
  2. Customs Clearance: Our agents process all necessary customs documentation for smooth export from Japan and import to your country.
  3. Vehicle Preparation: We prepare your vehicle for shipping according to international standards (battery disconnection, fuel levels, etc.).
  4. Loading & Shipping: Your vehicle is loaded using proper equipment and procedures to prevent damage during transit.
  5. Tracking & Updates: We provide regular updates and vessel tracking information throughout the shipping process.
  6. Arrival & Delivery: We coordinate with port agents to clear customs and arrange final delivery to your specified location.

Required Documents (We Handle These For You):

  • Original Title/Certificate of Ownership
  • Bill of Sale
  • Bill of Lading
  • Commercial Invoice
  • Packing List
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Insurance Certificate
  • Import Duty Documents
  • Customs Declaration Forms
  • Port Handling Documents
